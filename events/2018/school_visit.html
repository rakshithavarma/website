IEEE SB GVPCE(A)

Demonstration of Electronic Circuits to School Children

Date: 19 Feb 2018

Time: 10:30 AM to 02:00 PM

Event Category: Social Activity

Event Summary

The event was held on 19th February 2018 at ZPH School, Tallavalasa. It was organised by IEEE SB WIE GVPCE (A) supported by IEEE Vizag bay subsection. It was a technical demonstration given by B.Mohan dharmendra, Ch.Devi, D.Lakshmi narayanamma, B.Chinabala Revati of 3rd year ECE-1coordinated by Dr. V.Leela Rani, Student Advisor IEEE WIE GVPCE(A).

The event has begun at 10:30 AM. The schedule time was 3 hrs 30 min. from 10:30 A.M. to 2:00 P.M. The goal of the workshop is to motivate the school children towards technical knowledge enhancement. Live demonstration and presentation was given for electronic circuits like Mobile charger, automatic on-off lights and Water level indicator. Students are also motivated for doing good projects which will be useful for them in domestic activities.
