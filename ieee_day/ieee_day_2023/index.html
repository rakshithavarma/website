About IEEE Day

IEEE Day '23

Join us in celebrating IEEE Day 2023! Since 1884, we've united as a global community, committed to excellence. This year's theme is "EVOLVE: Elevate to Excellence," and our festivities run from September 26th to October 7th. Let's collaborate and harness technology for a brighter future! Together, we can shape the next era of innovation and progress. Join the movement and be a part of our global mission for a better tomorrow.

Motto for IEEE Day '23

IEEE Student Branch GVPCE(A) is gearing up for IEEE Day on October 7th, 2023, with the theme "Evolve - Elevate to Excellence." Join us in our journey of continuous growth and excellence. Our dedicated team is ready to initiate, motivate and coordinate inspiring events.We welcome you all to join us in making this journey remarkable!
